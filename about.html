Our story

Built at the loom, not around it.

Trueloomery grew out of a cotton and padded-outerwear factory — not a design studio looking for a factory to work with. That order matters, and it shapes everything we make.

We are Trueloomery — a cotton outerwear house built on real craftsmanship, not middlemen.

Every jacket starts at our own looms, where quality is controlled from yarn to finished garment. Comfort isn't an afterthought here; it's woven into every stitch.

For years, we manufactured for other people's labels. Trueloomery is what happens when we put our own name on the work — the same factory, the same standards, but with a point of view.

What we stand for

Three things we don't compromise on.

Everything else is negotiable. These aren't.

Source, not sourcing

We manufacture in our own facility. When you order from Trueloomery, you're one step from the loom, not three steps from a trading company.

Comfort as a spec

Hand-feel, weight, and warmth are treated as measurable requirements in every tech pack — not a marketing adjective added afterward.

Built to grow with you

Tiered MOQs mean a new brand and an established one can both start a program with us, and scale it as their business grows.
